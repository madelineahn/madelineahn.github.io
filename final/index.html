Amphibipedia connects research, education, and conservation by sharing information about amphibians to people around the world.

A healthy future for all life on Earth must include thriving amphibians. The means to conserving amphibians is to enable and facilitate better research and education with an accessible, consolidated, and curated information system for all amphibian species.

Interested in contributing to the Amphibipedia database? Visit our Account Guidelines page.

Privacy

Amphibipedia is an ongoing collaboration between researchers, citizen scientists, and students around the world led by the University of California, Berkeley. Amphibipedia is a work of fiction. Any names, places, and people used in Amphibipedia are not associated with their real-life counterparts.
