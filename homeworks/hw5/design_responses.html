  • In 4-5 sentences, please explain which of the inspirations were your favorite, why, and how the interfaces achieved their goal in a unique/delightful manner.

    Vlad Fedoseyev's shopping design is my favorite. I really like how the page loads in with its contents sliding in from all sides to achieve a smooth-feeling transition that draws the eye toward the center. The color/size selectors also have animations after being clicked, which makes the shopper feel satisfied after making a choice. Overall, the animations both draw and maintain your attention to that part of the page.
